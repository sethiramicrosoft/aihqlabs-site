About Sudhakar Ethirajulu

Technology, business, and transformation — all in one view.

I’m Sudhakar Ethirajulu, a Principal Program Manager at Microsoft with close to two decades of experience across cloud, AI, infrastructure, applications, data, security, enterprise architecture, and technology strategy.

My career has moved across systems engineering, consulting, product management, enterprise architecture, presales, sales leadership, and change leadership. That breadth helps me see technology from every angle: how it is built, positioned, adopted, and turned into value.

At Microsoft, I lead strategic initiatives across Commercial Marketplace, Modern Commerce, and AI-powered automation. My recent work includes AI-assisted proposal automation, multi-agent knowledge orchestration, and AI assistant capabilities in Azure Copilot and Partner Center.

I care about technology that helps people move forward clearly and confidently. My current focus is AI that simplifies processes, improves decision-making, and reduces operational burden instead of just sounding impressive.
